What Exactly Is The Coronavirus?

A Brief Covid-history

The Coronavirus originates from Wuhan, China. It spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a surface or object that has the virus on it, then touches their eyes, nose or mouth.

Stay at home If

  • You have symptoms of Coronavirus, you'll need to stay at home for 7 days.
  • You live with someone who has symptoms, you'll need to stay at home for 14 days from the day the first person in the home started having symptoms.
  • Your Parish is under restrictions.

If you live with someone who is 70 or over, has a long-term condition, is pregnant or has a weakened immune system, try to find somewhere else for them to stay for 14 days. If you have to stay at home together, try to keep away from each other as much as possible.

Coronavirus And Jamaica

The Coronavirus is in Jamaica and at this time 720 persons have contracted the virus.

Measures implemented to protect Jamaican Citizens:

  1. Schools have been suspended.
  2. Large gatherings of people (Parties, Weddings, Funerals etc.) have been suspended.
  3. Airports and seaports have been closed.
  4. Some BPOs have been closed.
  5. A hotline has been set up for persons who suspect that they have contracted the virus.
  6. An abundance of health care professionals has been acquired from Cuba. This includes 90 specialist nurses, along with doctors and therapists.
  7. Daily curfews.
  8. Specific communities have been quarantined.
  9. Other behind the scene work.

Keep Safe!

Can We Prevent The Spread Of The Coronavirus?

Yes! We can by practicing good hygiene.

How do you prevent Coronavirus?

  1. Wash your hands with soap and water often â€“ do this for at least 20 seconds.
  2. Always wash your hands when you get home or into work.
  3. Use hand sanitiser gel if soap and water are not available.
  4. Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your sleeve (not your hands) when you cough or sneeze.
  5. Put used tissues in the bin immediately and wash your hands afterwards.
  6. Avoid close contact with people who have symptoms of Coronavirus.
  7. Only travel on public transport if you need to.
  8. Work from home, if you can.
  9. Avoid social activities, such as going to bars, restaurants, theatres and cinemas.
  10. Do not touch your eyes, nose or mouth if your hands are not clean.
  11. Do not have visitors to your home, including friends and family.
